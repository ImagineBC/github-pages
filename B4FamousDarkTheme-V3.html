About B4Famous

B4Famous is a passionate collective of independent songwriters dedicated to promoting emerging musicians and artists through ImagineBC. United by a shared love for storytelling through music, we aim to amplify voices, foster creativity, and create meaningful connections between artists and audiences. Our members bring unique styles, backgrounds, and experiences, but together, we share one goal: to make music that resonates before fame finds us.

Meet Some of Our Artists

JudyAnne Jackson

With a voice that echoes timeless soul and lyrics that speak to the heart, Florida-based singer-songwriter JudyAnne has captivated audiences nationwide. A finalist on NBC’s Today Show and a PBS Celebration of Music awardee, she has performed at historic venues like The Bitter End in NYC. Her EPs Old Soul and Dear Me showcase her passionate storytelling and undeniable vocal talent.

Visit Her Page

Kyle Shaw

A storyteller at heart, Kyle left the corporate world to pursue music full-time. His 2023 self-titled debut album introduced his soulful voice and heartfelt lyrics to the Tampa Bay scene. With a second album on the way, Kyle continues to craft songs that move, inspire, and leave a lasting impression.

Visit His Page

CoAnn

A Berklee graduate, CoAnn seamlessly blends rock, jazz, and contemporary elements to create her genre-fusing sound. A singer, songwriter, producer, and wellness advocate, she weaves creativity and healing together through her brand, Dragonfly Holistics. Her EP CoAnn and music videos Paralyzed and Worlds Apart highlight her evolving artistry as she crafts her first full-length album.

Visit Her Page

Dean Johanesen

Inspired by speakeasy-era jazz, Western swing, and roots music, Dean merges history and Americana into his captivating storytelling. From Long Island to Ellenton, FL, he’s transporting listeners to another time. His latest album, Cautionary Tales, showcases his masterful songwriting and is available on all streaming platforms.

Visit His Page

Corey Hall

A torchbearer of American folk, Corey bridges past and present with his soulful voice and intricate guitar work. Equally adept at reviving old-time classics and penning original tunes, he brings authenticity and raw emotion wherever he performs.
